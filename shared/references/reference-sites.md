# Reference Sites — fetch and inspect at runtime

A study list of live sites whose copy patterns are worth learning from. Skills may
`web_fetch` these **at runtime** to study current structure and idiom.

> **Copyright rule (non-negotiable):** never copy a site's wording into output or into
> this repository. Fetch on demand to study *structure, idiom, and patterns*, then write
> original copy. Paraphrase mechanics ("they lead with a benefit + mechanism"), never
> sentences. The before/after examples in `examples/` are original and constructed.

The du/Sie verdicts below were verified June 2026 (see SOURCES.md and the German craft
references). Re-verify before relying on any single site — localized pages drift.

---

## German B2B SaaS — **Sie** register

Study how these stay warm and modern while using Sie (no Amtsdeutsch), how features
become Nutzen, sentence length, native CTAs, and compound spelling.

- **personio.de** — HR platform. CTA patterns "Demo anfragen" / "Demo vereinbaren".
- **hubspot.de** — CRM. Sie register localized from US copy without sounding translated.
- **pipedrive.com/de** — sales CRM. Watch concrete, verb-led value props.
- **deepl.com** (DE) — "KI-Lösungen, die Ihre Workflows transformieren"; "Vertrieb kontaktieren".

## German B2B SaaS — **du** register

Study informal-but-professional du voice and punchy imperatives.

- **sevdesk.de** — accounting. "Kostenlos starten"; segment + plain noun heroes.
- **lexware-office.de** — accounting. "Mit … läuft deine Buchhaltung fast von allein."
- **awork.com** (DE) — project mgmt. "Teste awork jetzt kostenlos!"
- **notion.com** (DE) — keeps du to protect an informal product voice.
- **slack.com** (de-de) — same; informal but precise.
- **traderepublic.com** — fintech. "Mach mehr aus deinem Geld."
- **n26.com** — neobank. "Die erste Onlinebank, die du lieben wirst."

**What to look for (both groups):** how a feature becomes a Nutzen; average sentence
length; absence of Floskeln; compound spelling (Cloud-Lösung, no Deppenleerzeichen);
native CTA wording; and — most important — *consistent* Anrede across the whole page.

## English B2B SaaS — widely regarded as strong copy

Treat as worth studying, **not** as gospel — judge each line for yourself.

- **stripe.com** — precise, developer-credible, benefit-first.
- **linear.app** — opinionated, confident, concrete.
- **vercel.com** — outcome-led with restraint.
- **basecamp.com** (37signals) — famously plain, benefit-led, anti-jargon.
- **intercom.com** — conversational and clear.
- **figma.com** — concrete value props, scannable structure.

**What to look for:** clarity, concrete value props, benefit-first heroes, restrained
tone, scannable structure, one idea per section.
